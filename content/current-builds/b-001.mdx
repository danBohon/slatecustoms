The Ryzen 5 5600X and RX 6700 XT is one of the best value combinations
you can put together on AM4 right now. The 5600X is still a capable
six-core processor that handles everything from competitive shooters to
open-world titles without breaking a sweat. Pair it with the 6700 XT —
a 12GB card that punches well above its price — and you have a machine
that eats 1080p and handles 1440p better than most builds in this range.

This one was built around parts sourced smart. The board, RAM, PSU, and
cooler came in under budget without cutting corners on what matters —
leaving room to put real money into the GPU and CPU where it counts.
The Sama V40 keeps things clean with solid airflow and enough room to
work in without being a full tower.

No Windows key is included. Licenses are easy to grab online and I didn't
want to pad the price with something you might already have.
