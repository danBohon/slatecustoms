This was my personal machine. It's on here because it represents exactly
what SLATE. builds are about — maximum performance, serious attention to
detail, and zero compromise on the things that matter.

The 9800X3D is the best gaming CPU AMD makes. The 3D V-Cache architecture
does something no clock speed bump can replicate — it keeps the CPU fed
with data so fast that it stops being the bottleneck in almost every
gaming scenario. Paired with the RX 9070 XT, a 16GB card that trades
blows with GPUs costing significantly more, this is an all-AMD build
that doesn't leave anything on the table.

The silent part is where this build separates itself from anything you'd
buy off a shelf. The Fractal North runs quiet by design — good airflow,
dampened panels, no sharp edges on the acoustic path. Noctua fans are
the best in the business for noise-normalized airflow, period. The Arctic
Liquid Freezer III Pro keeps the 9800X3D cool and near-silent even under
sustained load. The EVGA SuperNOVA 1000 P5 is fully modular and runs
clean — no coil whine, no fan noise at typical loads. The result is a
machine that you can hear yourself think next to, even when it's working
hard.

64GB DDR5-6000 and a PCIe 5.0 NVMe give this build headroom that most
prebuilts don't bother with. The platform is AM5 — AMD's current socket
with years of CPU upgrade path ahead.

This is what I build when I'm building for myself. Every SLATE. build
gets the same level of care.
